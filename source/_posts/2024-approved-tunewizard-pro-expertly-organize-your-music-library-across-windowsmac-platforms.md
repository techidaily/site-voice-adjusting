## TuneWizard Pro: Expertly Organize Your Music Library Across Windows/Mac Platforms (2024 Update)

ID3 is a metadata container widely used in conjunction with MP3 audio files. This container file is used to store information like artist, track number, album, etc. ID3 tags have two unrelated versions, ID3v1 and ID3v2\. ID3 tags are used for quick identification of the songs, without putting much effort or without even playing the song.

The tags consisting of the information helps in finding the song. If any song does not have ID3 tags, you can add metadata yourself for your ease. For this purpose, MP3 tag editors are available that can be used to add and edit the information. The following article will talk about music tag editors for both Windows and Mac users. So, let's begin!

This one is the most commonly encountered issue if you are working with the app in an Android smartphone. All you get to see is a blank screen that eventually ends up in the software crashing within a few seconds. To set the issue straight, you can try to reboot your device by simultaneously pressing the ‘Home’ and ‘Power’ keys for 10 seconds. This one is often a temporary error that results from weak or interrupted internet connectivity. Check your Wi-Fi plugins and mobile data connections to resolve the issue. Reducing the number of Wi-Fi users can also ensure a better connectivity.

You may encounter a login issue due to poor data connectivity, incorrect login credentials, glitches in third-party networking services and a deactivated or banned account.
