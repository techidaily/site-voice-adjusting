Why Do You Need An Audio Mixer For Streaming?

If youve ever wondered why some peoples audio sounds so much better than others when streaming, its likely because theyre using an audio mixer.

An audio mixer is a console that takes multiple audio signals and combines them into one signal. This allows you to control the individual levels of each sound, as well as add special effects like EQ and reverb.

While you dont necessarily need an audio mixer to stream, it can certainly make your life easier. So, if youre planning on doing any sort of professional streaming, this device is a must-have. However, they can be expensive, complicated to use, and sometimes add latency to your live audio.
